Do you remember the days when games were hard and made you think? /v/GA 2012 is our idea of bringing that back to the gaming community.

  • Launched: Nov 28, 2012
  • Funding ends: Jan 27, 2013
  • Remind Me

The /v/GAs (Vidya Gaem Awards) were born in early December 2011 as a response to the Spike VGAs, which were widely regarded as terrible and a cancer upon the video game industry.

A small group of anons decided to make their own presentation for /v/: after a month or so of planning, categories were made, votes were tallied and the presentation was shown to over 10,000 people live. It was widely regarded as terrible and a cancer upon /v/.

Despite this, there was a lot of positive feedback, and we felt that if we took the many suggestions we got on board that we could make something that all of /v/ could enjoy.

>get back to reddit
We want to make this explicitly clear: we do not want Reddit to be involved in any way, shape or form, and if you are visting this site from Reddit please fuck off and never come back. The same was true last year, we just never said it in so many words.

Everything we do is based on feedback from /v/. If you think anything we're doing is too Reddit-tier, then let us know.

The /v/GAs are run for /v/, by /v/. Despite popular belief, le Reddit is not involved at all and we go to great lengths to keep it that way.

We're always happy for people to get involved: there are only a few people really "in charge", and that's only the minimum necessary to ensure things don't fall completely to shit. Getting involved is as easy as posting in the forum, joining the Steam group or hopping into Steam chat. Just like anybody can make a thread on /v/, anybody can participate in the /v/GAs.

If you want to see what it's like, there's nothing better than to watch last year's presentation. Hundreds of people were involved throughout the process (not including all the people who voted).

Whether you love us or hate us, we look forward to your reactions in the upcoming months.

We are making an action RPG game name /v/GA 2012. This is a game that consists of a few elements from other great games. Our first goal is to get a full team because it is only I and one other person at the moment and we lack numbers to give our project full life. I am currently finding other great artist so that I can complete my team. Our second goal is to start a website to give a visual and details about our creation. This website will also allow us to build a relationship with our supporters and help us start a community for /v/GA 2012 where they can gain full access to in game videos, game music, concept art, updates, game demos and much more. The next goal is to start working on the game itself the story line has already been worked out as well as the setting of the world.
This game is based on /v/GA 2012 from the Greek Gods, The Sphinx and Pharaoh, The Minotaur and the Labyrinth, and these are a few characters we want to bring to life in our video game. We are taking stories from the past and giving them life in a video game world. We are also focusing on fun and challenging combat such as being able to hit and strike matters for example to slow him down you would be able to focus on a monsters leg to decapitate it. You will also be able to strike his eyes to blind him so he can no longer see you this are just a few things we will be putting into /v/GA 2012.
